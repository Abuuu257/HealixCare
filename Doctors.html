Doctors

Dr. Michael Thompson - Neurologist

Dr. Michael Thompson specializes in neurodegenerative diseases like Parkinson’s and Alzheimer’s. He graduated from Harvard Medical School and completed his fellowship at Johns Hopkins. Known for his research contributions and compassionate care, Dr. Thompson creates a supportive environment for his patients.

Dr. John Miller - Orthopedic Surgeon

Dr. John Miller is a renowned orthopedic surgeon specializing in joint replacements and sports injuries. He studied at the University of Heidelberg and completed his surgical residency in Munich. Dr. Miller’s innovative techniques help patients regain mobility and improve their quality of life.

Dr. Emily Garcia - Gastroenterologist

Dr. Emily Garcia is a leading gastroenterologist with expertise in digestive and liver conditions. She completed her medical training at the University of Barcelona and her residency at the Cleveland Clinic. Dr. Garcia is dedicated to improving gastrointestinal care through advanced endoscopic procedures.

Dr. David Collins - Thoracic Surgeon

Dr. David Collins is a thoracic surgeon specializing in surgeries of the lungs and esophagus. He trained at King’s College London and the Cleveland Clinic. Dr. Collins is highly regarded for his expertise in complex surgeries and his commitment to excellent patient care.

Dr. Thomas Rodriguez - Cardiologist

Dr. Thomas Rodriguez is an experienced cardiologist with over 15 years in heart care. He graduated from the University of Texas and completed his residency at the Mayo Clinic. Dr. Rodriguez specializes in heart failure management and advanced cardiac treatments.

Dr. Paul Anderson - Urologist

Dr. Paul Anderson is a urologist specializing in urinary tract and male reproductive system disorders. He trained at the University of Toronto and Johns Hopkins. Dr. Anderson is known for his meticulous surgical skills and compassionate approach to patient care.

Dr. Mark Evans - Endocrine Surgeon

Dr. Mark Evans is an endocrine surgeon focusing on surgeries of hormone-producing glands. He completed his medical degree at Stanford and his residency at the Cleveland Clinic. Dr. Evans is dedicated to performing precise surgeries and providing personalized care.

Dr. Luke Romano - Vascular Surgeon

Dr. Luke Romano is a vascular surgeon specializing in diseases of the arteries and veins. He trained at the University of Rome and the University of Chicago. Dr. Romano is known for his expertise in minimally invasive vascular surgeries and improving patient outcomes.

Dr. Ethan Brooks - Veterinarian

Dr. Ethan Brooks is a veterinarian focused on small animal care, including dogs, cats, and exotic pets. He earned his veterinary degree from the University of Sydney and trained at the Royal Veterinary College. Dr. Brooks is known for his gentle approach and expertise in preventive care and surgeries.
