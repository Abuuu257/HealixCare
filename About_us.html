About us

Welcome to HealixCare
  • At HealixCare, we are dedicated to delivering compassionate, high-quality healthcare tailored to each individual. Our mission is to provide personalized care that addresses both your physical health and emotional well-being. Guided by experienced professionals, we offer a comprehensive range of medical services, utilizing the latest advancements in technology and evidence-based practices. We prioritize a patient-centered approach, ensuring that your health journey is supported every step of the way.

  • Our healing environment respects your dignity and empowers you to take charge of your health. Welcome to HealixCare, where your well-being is our top priority.
HealixCare Central Hospital
The flagship branch of HealixCare, Central Hospital offers comprehensive medical services with a focus on advanced technology and specialty care. Located in the heart of Sri Lanka, it features state-of-the-art diagnostic facilities, a wide range of specialties, and expert medical professionals dedicated to providing top-notch care. Patients can access everything from emergency services to elective surgeries in a modern and supportive environment.
HealixCare Wellness Clinic
HealixCare Wellness Clinic is dedicated to preventive care and outpatient services. This branch focuses on routine health check-ups, chronic disease management, and lifestyle improvement programs. With a team of skilled healthcare providers, the clinic aims to promote overall well-being through personalized treatment plans and health education, ensuring that patients maintain their best health through proactive measures.
HealixCare Pediatric Center
The HealixCare Pediatric Center specializes in the medical care of children and adolescents. This branch provides a child-friendly environment designed to make healthcare experiences as comfortable and stress-free as possible. From routine vaccinations to specialized pediatric treatments, the center combines expert care with a compassionate approach, focusing on the unique needs of younger patients and their families.
